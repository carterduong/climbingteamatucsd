Perks of Joining

Discounted Gear

We are sponsored by FiveTen, Adidas Outdoor, and Prana. Our members get awesome discounts from these vendors.

Discounted Membership

Members on the team have access to a discounted membership from Mesa Rim, which is where we hold our practices.

Exclusive Access

The team also gets exclusive access to Mesa Rim's Training Facility once a week for Wednesday practices.
