About

Founded in 2015, The Climbing Team at UCSD seeks to not only train to compete in the Collegiate Climbing Series sponsored by USA Climbing, but to also promote a healthy lifestyle through rock climbing. The climbing community, both recreational and competitive, is extremely supportive and caring. We wish to strengthen that community within UC San Diego so that it can attract a more diverse student body.

What We Do

Outdoor Climbing

The climbing team frequently goes on outdoor climbing trips to amazing locations like Bishop, Red Rocks, and Joshua Tree.

Weekly Practice

The team has weekly practices at the local climbing gym, where members get to hang out and climb together.

Competitions

Team members also get the chance to compete in collegiate competitions, as well as local competitions happening in the area.
