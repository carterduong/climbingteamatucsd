About

Founded in 2015, The Climbing Team at UCSD seeks to not only train to compete in the Collegiate Climbing Series sponsored by USA Climbing, but to also promote a healthy lifestyle through rock climbing. The climbing community, both recreational and competitive, is extremely supportive and caring. We wish to strengthen that community within UC San Diego so that it can attract a more diverse student body.
