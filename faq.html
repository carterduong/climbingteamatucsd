FAQs
The team is open to members of all climbing levels. We have a mix of experience levels ranging from those who have never climbed before all the way to those with over ten years of experience.
There is no tryout for the team, anyone is allowed to join. There is no cap on the amount of members, so we accept everyone who meets the requirements.
Team members are not required to go to competitions, many of our members join for the casual community aspect of the team.
We have a carpool system so that our members can make it to practice even if they do not have a car. We set up rides on our Facebook page every week, so make sure to join the group if you want a ride.
With the proper precautions and safety knowledge, rock climbing is no more dangerous than any other sport. Feel free to ask any of our experienced members about how to stay safe while climbing!
